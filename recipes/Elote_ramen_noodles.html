Elote Ramen Noodles

Description

Elote Ramen noodles is the instant ramen recipe you absolutely have to try. It is a mixture of classic instant ramen and the flavors of Mexican street corn. Cheese, chili powder, lime juice, and mayonnaise are typically combined and spread over ears of corn making a flavor combination that’s out of this world.

Take your everyday instant ramen noodles to the next level with this viral recipe for Elote Ramen! Taking Tik Tok by storm, Elote Ramen is smothered in a rich, creamy, cheesy sauce with plenty of spice and loads of flavor. You’ll never make instant ramen the same way again!

Ingredients

  • 2 ears fresh corn, husked
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot sauce, such as Valentina
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 cup half-and-half
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 3 (3 ounce) packages instant ramen noodles, seasoning packets discarded
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 1/4 cup crumbled cotija cheese, or as needed (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro, or as needed (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Heat an indoor or outdoor grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Add corn and jalapeno directly to the grate. Grill 5 minutes. Turn and grill 5 minutes more.
  3. Transfer to a cutting board. Cut corn off the cob. Seed and chop the jalapeno. Set aside.
  4. Whisk mayonnaise and hot sauce together. Set aside.
  5. Heat water, half-and half, and chili powder in a large skillet over medium heat. Add ramen and simmer for 2 minutes. Flip ramen over and cook until noodles are broken up, about 2 minutes more.
  6. Stir in mozzarella cheese and cook until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.
  7. Stir in corn and jalapeno.
  8. Transfer to serving dishes; top with mayonnaise mixture. Garnish with lime wedges, cotija, and cilantro.
